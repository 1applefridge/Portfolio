Who I Am

I'm a multidisciplinary designer-developer with a passion for creating intuitive digital experiences. My journey began in visual arts, evolved through interaction design studies, and now thrives at the intersection of aesthetics and functionality.

What drives me is solving complex problems with elegant solutions. I believe good design should be invisible, and technology should empower rather than complicate. When I'm not pushing pixels or debugging code, you'll find me exploring the mountains or experimenting with film photography.

Currently, I'm focused on designing accessible web applications while exploring the potential of augmented reality interfaces. I thrive in collaborative environments where design and development teams work symbiotically.
